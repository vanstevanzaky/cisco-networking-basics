### 1. Jenis Jaringan Wireless

| Jenis | Jangkauan | Contoh Penggunaan |
|-------|-----------|-------------------|
| **WPAN** | ~10m | Bluetooth, Zigbee |
| **WLAN** | ~100m | Wi-Fi (802.11) |
| **WMAN** | Kota | WiMAX |
| **WWAN** | Global | 4G LTE, 5G |

### 2. Komponen WLAN

- **Wireless NIC** - adapter Wi-Fi pada client
- **Wireless Access Point (AP)** - bridge ke wired network
- **Wireless Router** - AP + router + switch dalam 1 device

## üì° Wi-Fi Standards (IEEE 802.11)

| Standard | Frekuensi | Max Speed | Nama Marketing |
|----------|-----------|-----------|----------------|
| 802.11a | 5 GHz | 54 Mbps | - |
| 802.11b | 2.4 GHz | 11 Mbps | - |
| 802.11g | 2.4 GHz | 54 Mbps | - |
| 802.11n | 2.4/5 GHz | 600 Mbps | Wi-Fi 4 |
| 802.11ac | 5 GHz | 6.9 Gbps | Wi-Fi 5 |
| 802.11ax | 2.4/5/6 GHz | 9.6 Gbps | Wi-Fi 6/6E |

**Poin Penting:**
- **2.4 GHz** - jangkauan lebih jauh, lebih crowded
- **5 GHz** - lebih cepat, jangkauan pendek, less interference
